The full picture

I got into data because I genuinely believe information is one of the most underused tools most organizations have. Not dashboards for the sake of dashboards — but the right information, at the right time, in a form that actually helps someone make a better call. That's what I've spent ten years trying to build.

Most of my work sits somewhere between the technical and the human side of that problem. I build the pipelines and architectures — FHIR and OMOP data models, ETL systems, forecasting infrastructure — but I've always been just as interested in whether the person on the other end can actually use what I built. Being the bridge between a complex data system and someone who needs to make a decision with it is where I feel most useful.

One of the moments I'm most proud of professionally didn't involve a sophisticated algorithm. At Quest Diagnostics I had never used UiPath before, picked it up, and spent several months building a full RPA reporting infrastructure through trial and error. Watching something you taught yourself from scratch actually work — and watching it save people real time — is a different kind of satisfaction than hitting a metric.

Outside of my day job I keep building. DeepTime Studio started because I wanted to see if I could take the forecasting interpretability research I published at WPI and turn it into something anyone could interact with. The projects I build are the same thing I've always been drawn to: taking something complicated and making it genuinely useful.

DeepTime Studio
DEEP LEARNING TIME SERIES FORECASTING PLATFORM
● Live

A full-stack deep learning application for time series forecasting and anomaly detection. Upload any time series CSV and the app trains a PyTorch LSTM model, evaluates performance with MAE and RMSE metrics, generates future forecasts, detects anomalies via Z-score analysis, and delivers natural-language explanations of model behavior powered by Groq LLMs. Directly extends the published ISSAT research on time series forecasting interpretability into a live, interactive tool anyone can use.
